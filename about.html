About The Verdict on AI

A platform for South African attorneys navigating the AI revolution — practical, honest, and grounded in the realities of our legal profession.

Mphoentle Ndaba

Attorney · Corporate & Commercial · AI Practitioner

I've spent years practising law across two continents — including four years in the US legal field, where I watched AI debut and begin reshaping the profession firsthand. One thing has become clear: AI is no longer the future — it's here.

The Verdict on AI is my platform to help attorneys understand what that means for our profession. I break down judgments, AI tools, ethical obligations, and regulatory updates in a way that's practical, actionable, and relevant to South African law. My goal? To make sure lawyers don't just keep up — they stay ahead.

Why This Platform Exists

South African lawyers are facing a rapidly shifting landscape. AI tools are being adopted by firms of all sizes, courts are already encountering AI-related misconduct, and regulatory bodies are beginning to respond. Yet the conversation in SA legal circles often lags behind what's actually happening on the ground.

The Verdict on AI exists to close that gap. Every article is written with a practising attorney in mind — someone who needs to understand not just what AI is doing, but what it means for their practice, their clients, and their professional obligations.

What You'll Find Here

Case law analysis of AI-related judgments from SA and abroad. Practical breakdowns of AI tools used in legal practice. Guidance on POPIA compliance, ethical obligations, and risk management. Updates from the LPC, LSSA, and other regulatory bodies.
